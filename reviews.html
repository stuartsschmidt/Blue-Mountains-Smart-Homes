Son★★★★★June 2025
We had a wonderful stay at the Smart Retreat! The place was clean, well-maintained, and thoughtfully set up for families. We especially appreciated the handy selection of kids’ toys, board games, a TV, and a projector, which made relaxing together easy and enjoyable. Comfort features like the reclining couch, electric blankets, and a well-equipped kitchen added to the homely feel. The smart home setup with voice-activated controls was a fun and modern bonus. While a few devices had low batteries, it wasn’t a major inconvenience. A couple of things to keep in mind: the entrance has stairs, so it may not suit those with limited mobility, and the driveway is sloped, which could be a concern for low-clearance vehicles — but there’s ample street parking nearby. Stuart was very friendly and prompt in responding to our questions. Overall, it was a great retreat and a very comfortable family stay.
