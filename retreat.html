King Room

Queen Room

Google Mini and voice control make the room both smart and playful—perfect for young guests to relax and enjoy.

Living Room

Step into the living room and experience the perfect blend of comfort and technology. Sink into a luxurious three-seater electric recliner, complete with built-in lighting, Bluetooth speakers, and both wireless and USB charging for your devices. Two single electric recliners also feature built-in lighting and USB charging for added convenience. Kitchen

Step into a kitchen designed for both creativity and comfort. A large serving window opens the space to the outdoors, while engineered stone bench tops provide a stylish and durable workspace. Prepare meals with ease using modern appliances: oven, fridge with plumbed-in ice maker and chilled water dispenser (just fill the tank), microwave, coffee machine, toaster, kettle, and dishwasher. Choose from 3–4 ways to make your perfect coffee. Enjoy thoughtful touches like a built-in dishwashing soap dispenser, sensor tap, and motion-sensor bins for effortless clean-up. Smart lighting, Google Mini, and voice or remote-controlled electric blinds bring convenience and ambiance to every moment. All cookware, utensils, and cooking basics are ready for you—just bring your appetite and enjoy!

Dining Room

Share delicious meals and great conversation in the inviting dining room. A spacious table and plush seating create the perfect setting for family feasts or friendly gatherings. Set the mood with music or get help from the Google Mini, and enjoy effortless control of the smart lighting and electric curtain—just use your voice or the remote. Bathroom

Indulge in a spa-like experience in the main bathroom. Enjoy a smart toilet with heated seat, bidet, and remote control for easy use. Refresh in the voice-controlled Moen smart shower, or use the intuitive control panel for hands-on operation. The large, deep bath features relaxing jets for the perfect soak. The room offers two light settings, smart lighting, heaters, and a sensor tap for added comfort and convenience, while a smart electric mirror, hair dryer, and ample power points make getting ready a breeze. You'll find body wash, shampoo, conditioner, and soap provided for your comfort, along with a hair dryer and a smart electric mirror. Veranda

Step out onto the inviting veranda, where comfort and convenience meet the outdoors. A large serving window connects directly to the kitchen, making it easy to share food and drinks with guests outside. Relax in comfortable seating with beautiful garden views, or fire up the BBQ—complete with a rotisserie feature, built-in lights, and USB charging—for memorable alfresco meals. Whether you’re enjoying a quiet morning coffee or hosting an evening gathering, the veranda is the perfect spot to soak up the fresh air and peaceful surroundings.

Pet Friendly

Your pets are truly welcome at the retreat! Pet bowls are provided for your furry friends, and there is easy access to outdoor areas for walks and fresh air. Please supervise pets at all times and bring any bedding or other items your pet may need to feel comfortable during your stay.
