Welcome to the Future of Family Holidays

We’re Stuart and Lina, the proud “mum and dad” team behind Blue Mountains Smart Homes, located in beautiful Katoomba, Blue Mountains. Our mission? To bring a little bit of tomorrow to your holiday today—without losing the warmth, comfort, and fun of a true family getaway.

Who Are We?

We’re not a faceless corporation—we’re a family, just like yours! We’ve poured our hearts (and a few late-night cups of tea) into creating two of the most unique holiday homes in Katoomba, Blue Mountains. We look after our homes directly—no middle people, no agencies—so you always get a personal touch and genuine care from the owners themselves. Our homes are packed with smart features, from voice-controlled lighting and climate to entertainment systems that even the kids can master (with a little help from Google).

We believe holidays should be easy, fun, and a little bit magical. That’s why we’ve written detailed, friendly house manuals—so you can spend less time figuring out the tech and more time relaxing in the spa, watching a movie, or exploring the World Heritage wilderness just outside your door. (And don’t worry—if the house starts talking back, it’s just Google saying hi!)
